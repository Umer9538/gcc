## Survey Distribution Notes

### Google Forms Setup Instructions:

1. **Create New Form**: Go to Google Forms and create a new form titled "GCC Connect Platform - Requirements Survey"

2. **Add Introduction**:
   ```
   Welcome! We are developing the GCC Connect platform to improve communication and collaboration across our organization. Your feedback is crucial to ensuring this platform meets your needs. This survey will take approximately 10-15 minutes to complete. All responses are confidential and will be used solely for requirements gathering.
   ```

3. **Section Organization**: Use the section breaks as provided above to organize questions into logical groups

4. **Required Questions**: Mark these as required:
   - Questions 1, 2 (Basic info)
   - Questions 38-40 (Priorities and pain points)

5. **Question Types**:
   - Multiple choice: Single select questions
   - Checkboxes: "Select all that apply" questions
   - Linear scale: Rating questions (1-5 or 1-10)
   - Paragraph: Open-ended questions

6. **Bilingual Option**: Consider creating two versions:
   - English version
   - Arabic version (translate all questions)

7. **Distribution**:
   - Share via email to all employees
   - Include deadline (suggest 2 weeks)
   - Send reminder after 1 week
   - Share link in internal communication channels

8. **Response Analysis**:
   - Export responses to Google Sheets
   - Analyze patterns and priorities
   - Create summary report for stakeholders
   - Identify common pain points
   - Prioritize features based on responses

### Expected Outcomes:

- Feature prioritization based on actual user needs
- Understanding of current pain points
- Identification of most critical workflows
- User preferences for UI/UX decisions
- Training needs assessment
- Security requirements validation
- Mobile access requirements
- Language preferences
- Adoption readiness assessment
